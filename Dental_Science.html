About Dental Science

Dental science is the branch of medicine that is involved in the study, diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of diseases, disorders, and conditions of the oral cavity.

It’s much more than just filling cavities or pulling teeth; it’s about understanding the entire ecosystem of the mouth and how it connects with the rest of the body.
