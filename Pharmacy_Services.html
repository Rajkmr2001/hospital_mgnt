Take Charge of Your Health

Informed patients make better health decisions. Explore essential health facts and tips from our experts.

Know Your Medication

Always complete your course of antibiotics, even if you feel better. Stopping early can cause resistance and relapse.

Prevent Infections

Handwashing is the simplest and most effective way to prevent the spread of infections. Do it frequently and thoroughly.

Stay Hydrated

Drinking enough water daily helps with digestion, kidney function, and energy levels. Aim for 2–3 liters a day.
