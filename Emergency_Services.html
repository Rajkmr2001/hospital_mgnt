Emergency Preparedness Tips

Being prepared can save lives in emergency situations. Here are some essential tips from our emergency team.

First Aid Kit

Keep a well-stocked first aid kit at home and in your car. Include bandages, antiseptic, pain relievers, and emergency contact numbers.

CPR Knowledge

Learn basic CPR. Immediate CPR can double or triple chances of survival after cardiac arrest.

Emergency Signs

Know the warning signs of heart attack, stroke, and other emergencies to act quickly when they occur.
